What to Bring to Your DOT Physical

Come prepared and we'll have you certified and back on the road as quickly as possible.

  • Valid government-issued photo ID
  • Current eyeglasses or contact lenses (if applicable)
  • List of current medications
  • Medical records for any conditions requiring clearance
  • Hearing aids (if you use them)
  • CPAP compliance data (if required for sleep apnea)
