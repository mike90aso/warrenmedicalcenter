Primary Care/Infections
Adult Primary Care

Infections

When an infection is slowing you down, timely evaluation matters. Our primary care team treats common bacterial and viral illnesses for adults and helps you know when watchful waiting, medication, or further testing is appropriate.

Schedule Online Call (313) 945-8210

What We Help With

Common concerns we address in primary care for adults at Warren Medical Center:

  • Sinus, ear, throat, and upper respiratory infections
  • Urinary tract infections (UTIs)
  • Skin infections and cellulitis concerns
  • Bronchitis and cough-related illness
  • Fever and flu-like symptoms
  • Follow-up after recent infection or hospitalization

How We Approach Care

Same-day appointments may be available when slots are open. If you are experiencing severe symptoms such as trouble breathing or chest pain, seek emergency care.
