Adult Primary Care

Preventive Care

Preventive care is one of the best investments you can make in your health. At Warren Medical Center, we help adults stay ahead of problems with wellness exams, screenings, vaccinations, and practical lifestyle guidance.

Schedule Online Call (313) 945-8210

What We Help With

Common concerns we address in primary care for adults at Warren Medical Center:

  • Annual physicals and wellness exams
  • Age- and risk-based cancer and disease screenings
  • Vaccinations and immunization review
  • Blood work and routine lab monitoring
  • Blood pressure, BMI, and cardiovascular risk checks
  • Personalized prevention plans you can stick with

How We Approach Care

Bring your medication list and any recent records to your visit so we can build a complete picture of your health.
